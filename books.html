Books

At Solutions Way, our mission is to enhance the global impact and success of academic presses by offering a comprehensive publishing platform tailored to diverse needs. We support a variety of digital book formats, including monographs, handbooks, and textbooks, ensuring each title is presented in its finest form.



Our publishing partners rely on us to deliver excellence through customized solutions and extensive
distribution networks. Leveraging our strong connections with vendors, discovery services, and indexing
platforms, we ensure that every book achieves global visibility and reaches its target audience.

To further expand this influence, we actively promote book collections to libraries, broadening the presence
of open-access titles while driving sales of paid-access books. Our advanced infrastructure, featuring
institutional access via IP authentication and user-friendly navigation, ensures that your publications are
easily discoverable, maximizing their impact on the academic community.



Publishing Models

We offer flexible options tailored to the unique requirements and publishing strategies:

           Open Access – Enhance the visibility of your books by making them freely accessible


           Paid Access – Increase revenue through traditional sales channels


           Integrated Model – Combine both approaches for balanced reach and profitability
