Parents' and children's views

Mara Allodi Westling and Tamara Zappaterra
Publisher: LUDI COST Action

Play, disability; parents' and children's views. The needs of children
and parents about play when the child has a disability are explored
through surveys to disability associations and families were
collected during 2016 in 30 countries by members of the EU COST
LUDI network Play for children with disabilities. The users' needs
concerning play for children with disabilities are also explored by
mean of case studies at a country level, based on literature reviews
of available reports and empirical studies in Finland, Lithuania, and
Sweden.
