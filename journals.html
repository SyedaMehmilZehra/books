Journals

With over 20 years of experience in academic publishing, Solutions Way has become the premier partner for journals striving to excel in the dynamic and ever-changing research landscape. Our mission is to empower scholarly publications by providing the expertise, excellence, and flexibility that editors and publishers need to stay ahead. From enhancing editorial workflows to increasing visibility, we are dedicated to ensuring your journal's success with the utmost care and precision.

Exceptional Customer Support


At Solutions Way, we prioritize outstanding customer care, providing personalized assistance and
direct communication through multiple channels. Each partner is assigned a dedicated
contract coordinator to ensure seamless collaboration, swift issue resolution, and complete
satisfaction. Our customer service is the cornerstone of our success, reflecting our deep
commitment to fully supporting our partners.




Partner Publishing Program

Solutions Way partners with academic institutions to amplify their
publishing impact through effective portfolio distribution. Our
program features flexible pricing, editorial assistance and a
comprehensive range of services designed to support both new
and existing titles.
